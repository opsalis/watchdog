Why Watchdog?

4 Continents, Simultaneously

Every check runs from North America, Europe, and Asia at the same time. If your site is down in Asia but up in Europe, we detect it instantly.

Regional Outage Detection

Most monitors check from one location. We use majority consensus across all nodes — no false alarms, no missed outages.

Multi-Protocol Checks

HTTP, HTTPS, TCP, DNS, and SSL certificate monitoring. Track response times, status codes, and certificate expiry from every location.

Smart Alerting

Webhook, email, and Telegram notifications. Alerts fire only on state transitions — no alert fatigue from repeated pings.

Public Status Pages

Share a live status dashboard with your users. Embed it on your site or link from your footer. Always up to date.

Pay with USDC

No credit cards, no subscriptions that auto-renew. Pay in USDC for exactly what you need. Simple, transparent pricing.
